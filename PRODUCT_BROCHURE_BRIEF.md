## 2. The Problem (half page)

Short intro paragraph: HR teams juggle attendance, payroll, leave, recruitment, and compliance across spreadsheets and disconnected tools — leading to errors, delays, and poor employee experience.

## 3. The Solution — Product Overview

NestHR is an all-in-one Human Resource Management System with a web admin dashboard and a mobile app (iOS/Android) for employees, covering the full employee lifecycle from recruitment to exit.

## 6. Why Choose NestHR (differentiators)

- All-in-one platform — no more juggling 5 different tools
- Real-time attendance & biometric integration
- Automated payroll with configurable salary structures
- WhatsApp-based notifications/document delivery
- Role-based access with full audit trail
- Built for scale — company, department & designation hierarchy support
